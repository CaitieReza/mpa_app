There are three files in this folder:
1) Mobility and Growth Rates.csv
2) Mobility Classifications.pdf
3) References Mobility and Growth Rate Database.pdf


* "Mobility and Growth Rates.csv" - contains the species and family name of fish and invertebrates used in our analysis, common name, growth rate (r), relative mobility values, mobility classification, data source of the mobility values, and notes.

* Use the "Mobility Classifications.pdf" to interpret the mobility values in the "Mobility and Growth Rates.csv"

* The "References Mobility and Growth Rate Database.pdf" contains the references cited in the "Movement information notes and references" column of the "Mobility and Growth Rates.csv" file
